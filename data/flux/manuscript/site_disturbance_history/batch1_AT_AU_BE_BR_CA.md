## Notes on Data Quality

- **AT-Mmg, AT-Nsd**: Austrian sites with very limited web documentation. Likely new or minor sites added after FLUXNET2015. Recommend querying `https://www.europe-fluxdata.eu/home/site-details?id=AT-Mmg` and `AT-Nsd` for authoritative metadata.
- **BR-ITA**: No AmeriFlux page retrievable; FLUXNET metadata only. Recommend checking `https://ameriflux.lbl.gov/sites/siteinfo/BR-ITA` directly.
- **CA-CF3, CA-DSM, CA-KLP, CA-LU1, CA-LU2, CA-PB1, CA-PB2**: Newer Canadian AmeriFlux sites with limited published documentation. Coordinates are approximate from regional context.
- **AU-Fle**: OzFlux site with limited online description at time of compilation. Recommend OzFlux data portal.
